Boston Celtics

Team Info: The Boston Celtics are part of the National Basketball Association where they have been a historic franchise that has won more championships(17) than any other team in the leauge. Bill Russel was a key players in the early years of the team, who led them to 11 championships during his 13 year career. The Celtics are now led by Coach Brad Stevens.

Previous Season: The Boston Celtics lost in game 5 of the Conference Semi Finals to the Milwaukee Bucks.

Key Players: Kemba Walker(G), Jayson Taytum(F), Jaylen Brown(F), Gordon Hayward(F).

Championships: 1957, 1959, 1960, 1961, 1962, 1963, 1964, 1965, 1966, 1968, 1969, 1974, 1976, 1981, 1984, 1986, 2008.
