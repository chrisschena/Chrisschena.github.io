Boston Sports Teams

New England Patriots

Patriots: The New England Patriots are a Football team in the NFL that have won 6 Superbowls in the last 20 years. They are lead by Tom Brady at quarterback and Bill Belichick at coach. They have been the best at what they do for their entire careers, and have made famous the slogan "Do Your Job".

Boston Bruins

Bruins: The Boston Bruins are an original 6 team in the NHL which is now comprised of 30 teams. They are led by their Captain Zdeno Chara (one of the tallest proffesional hockey players ever at 6'9"), Brad Marchand, & Patrice Bergeron. The Boston Bruins most recently one the Stanley Cup in 2011 since then they have gone back twice and lost, including last years game 7 loss to the St. Lous Blues.
