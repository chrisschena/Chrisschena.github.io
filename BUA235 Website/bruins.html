Boston Bruins

Team Info: The Boston Bruins are an original 6 team in the National Hockey Leauge which is now comprised of 30 teams. They are led by their Coach Bruce Cassidy and their Captain Zdeno Chara (one of the tallest proffesional hockey players ever at 6'9"). The Boston Bruins earned the nickname "The Big Bad Bruins" for their rough style of play in the 70's and 80's.

Previous Season: The Boston Bruins lost in game 7 of the Stanley Cup Finals to the St. Louis Blues.

Key Players: Patrice Bergeron(C), Zdeno Chara (D), Brad Marchand(LW), Tukka Rask(G).

Championships: 1929, 1939, 1941, 1970, 1972, 2011.
