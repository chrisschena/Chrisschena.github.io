New England Patriots

Team Info: The New England Patriots are a football team in the National Football LEauge that have won 6 Superbowls in the last 20 years. The turnaround of the Patriots franchise is largely acredited to Quarterback Tom Brady and Coach Bill Belichick who have made famous the slogan "Do Your Job".

Previous Season: The Patriots won the Superbowl this past season against the LA Rams by a score of 13-3.

Key Players: Tom Brady(QB), Julian Edelman(WR), Devin Mcourty(S).

Championships: 2001 (XXXVI), 2003 (XXXVIII), 2004 (XXXIX), 2014 (XLIX), 2016 (LI), 2018 (LIII).
