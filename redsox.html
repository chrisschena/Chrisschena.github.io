Boston Redsox

Team Info: The Boston Redsox are a premier team in Major Leauge Baseball who are most famous for the "Curse of the Big Bambino". This Curse is supposedly the reason the Redsox didnt wint a World Series from 1918-2004 (86 years). Since the curse was broken they have now won 4 World Series in the past 15 years. They are now coached by Manager Alex Cora.

Previous Season: The Boston Redsox failed to make the playoffs this past season.

Key Players: Chris Sale(P), Mookie Betts(RF), Xander Bogaerts(SS).

Championships: 1903, 1912, 1915, 1916, 1918, 2004, 2007, 2013, 2018.
